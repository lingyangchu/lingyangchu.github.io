Lingyang Chu

I am a Principal Researcher at Huawei Technologies Canada, Co., Ltd.

Address: 4321 Still Creek Dr, Burnaby, BC, Canada, V5C 6S7.

Research. My research interest is in the areas of data mining, machine learning and statistics. I am especially interested in designing novel algorithms to interpret deep neural networks and to defense against adversarial attacks. I am also an expert in designing large scale dense subgraph detection algorithms with solid theoretical guarantees.

Recruiting. I am recruiting highly motivated researchers, engineers and research interns to conduct cutting-edge research on interpreting deep models, defending against adversarial attacks and building next-generation reliable computing frameworks at Huawei Vancouver Research Centre. Feel free to contact me.
