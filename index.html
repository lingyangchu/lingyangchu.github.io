Principal Researcher at Huawei Technologies Canada, Co., Ltd.

Address: 8888 University Drive, Burnaby, BC, Canada, V5A 1S6.

My primary research interest is in the areas of data mining, machine learning and statistics. I am especially interested in designing novel algorithms to interpret deep neural networks and to defense against adversarial attacks. I am also experienced in designing large scale dense subgraph detection algorithms with solid theoretical guarantees.

I am hiring highly motivated researchers, engineers and Ph.D student interns to conduct cutting-edge research on deep model interpretation, adversarial defense and reliable computing at Huawei Vancouver Research Centre.

Biography

I have been leading several research projects as a principal researcher at Huawei Vancouver Research Centre since April 2019.

I worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow under the supervision of Prof. Jian Pei at Simon Fraser University from September 2015 to April 2019.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in June 2015.

I received my B.Sc. degree in Communication Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in June 2009.
