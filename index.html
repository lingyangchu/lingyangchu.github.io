Lingyang Chu

Ph.D.

I will join McMaster University as an assistant professor on January 1st, 2021. I am currently a Principal Researcher at Huawei Technologies Canada, Co., Ltd. Before joining Huawei, I successfully led 7+ research projects as a Postdoctoral Fellow supervised by Prof. Jian Pei at Simon Fraser University from September 2015 to November 2018. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in June 2015.

Research Interest

My research interest is in the areas of data mining, machine learning and statistics. I am especially interested in designing novel algorithms to interpret deep neural networks and to defense against adversarial attacks. I am also an expert in designing large scale dense subgraph detection algorithms with solid theoretical guarantees.
